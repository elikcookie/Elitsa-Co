Hamster Care Resources


Cage

The most important thing to think about when choosing which hamster cage you want to buy is the size.
Hamsters may be small but they need a lot of space. Legal minumum hamster cage requirements vary but what we should be aiming for is the German minimum requirements which are:
Minimum Floorspace: 1550 sqin / 10000sqcm
Minimum Dimensions: 40x40x24 in / 100x100x60 cm
And if you can get a cage bigger than that that's even better for the hamster.

You can either make your own cage, search for diy bin-cages or diy ikea hamster cages, or you can buy a pre-made one. Just make sure the size is good and that it is not made from harmful materials.

Food

One big misconception about hamsters is that they only eat plant foods, which is not the case.
Hamsters are in fact omnivores and deciding on what the best diet for your hamster can be a very complicated topic as usually hamster mixes sold in stores are not complete,
and a hamster owner would need to add other mixes and other foods to a mix to make it complete.
You can even make your own hamster mix but that requires a lot of research and can be a lot more expnesive.
